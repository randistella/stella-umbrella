The Story Behind Stella Umbrella

In August 2022, a woman was killed by an umbrella driven by the wind in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. That day, I came up with a design for umbrella canopies, which would be the world's first and only patented flightless umbrella.

Since 2022 we have gone through wind tunnel and real-world testing, and we have now officially partnered with East Coast Umbrella in North Carolina. They have been building quality umbrellas since 1986.

We are now ready to sell and market an umbrella that can withstand bursts of wind in excess of 50 mph.

We believe there is no need for 3000 people a year to be injured by flying umbrellas; the solution is the Stella Umbrella.

In the past few days, another woman was killed by a market umbrella in South Carolina. These incidents can now be prevented.

Share this information to help us promote our product and save people from being hurt or killed.
